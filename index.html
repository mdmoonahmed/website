About Bangladesh

Bangladesh is a country in South Asia, bordered by India to the west, north, and east, Myanmar to the southeast, and the Bay of Bengal to the south. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, diverse landscapes, and vibrant history.

In this travel vlog, we explore the beautiful region of Sylhet, famous for its lush tea gardens, rolling hills, and serene natural beauty. Join us as we discover the hidden gems of this enchanting destination.
